Ramen Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scramble your tastebuds with this simple yet delicious recipe! Quick & Easy!

Ingredients..

  • 1 (3 oz) package ramen noodles (any flavor)
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh parsley, or to taste
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Â½ onion, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp water, or as desired
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water; add ramen noodles & cook for 3 min. Drain water and stir seasoning packet & parsley into noodles.
  2. Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat; cook & stir onion until fragrant, about 2 min. Add noodles & stir for 2 more min.
  3. Whisk eggs, water, and salt together in a bowl; pour into the same skillet as the noodle mixture. Cook & stir eggs until set and cooked through, 2 to 4 min.
